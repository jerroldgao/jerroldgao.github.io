Education Reflection

The education in Iowa State University is driving me to learn more things from outside resources and filling my urge of solving challenge problems.
The most exciting in Iowa State is that I find the path I could donate my passion to. I was an applied physics students become I transferred to computer engineering. Though I felt achievement from solving equation, deducting theory and comfirming correctness of theory in lab, I could not certain if I could continue to do this in future career. My advisor asked me to do self-discovery. After struggling for several months, I finally realized that what I care about is saving time and work by optimizing process. The ultramate time saving tool is no doubt – computer. So I tranferred to computer engineering.
However, computer engineering is such a large and broad area just like an ocean which can get you easily drowned. To get ahead, I seeked for more resources to look at and guides from professors. Fortunately, due to my lab assistant experience in Iowa State MEMS lab works with Dr.Dong, I got enough research skill to search the information I want. I pooled high quality paper, interesting projects and creditable developers’ blogs into my own database prepared for more challenge problems.
The internship in Litsoft helps me to know about product process in industry area. Litsoft is subbranch of Lenovo, focus on creating communication service in medical area. I joined to design, programming and review my collegue’s code and noticed that readablility is far more important than the simplisity of the code. Because if there is any maintenance or bug fixing in the future, anyone who join to the position can understand your code.
Since 2012 Big data has been showing up on the stage, which boosted neural network and machine learning application development. In 2015, I found resources and the way to keep my information update. After a short period, my teammates and I create a mobile app to record learning progress of autistic people and provide visualized data to their doctor or guardian. Coincidentally, I got my senior design project which requires us to created argumented communication system for autisitic people. An intuitive way I choose is combined natural language processing with machine learning to help discover user preference. In March 2016, AlphaGo beats Lee Sedol by 4:1 in Go game. I luckily know some people who work in those areas and read papers they wrote for how to create multi-layers filter system to reduce the search in learning process.
Around June, all kinds of machine learning tool such as API.AI, Tensorflow has been published. Unfortunately, those tools need a tremendous computation power to make learning process. I found Dr.Zembreno who works with GPU architecture, and he allow me to use one desktop and test my recognition program.
The general education I took in Iowa State let me know more about social structure and economy.
